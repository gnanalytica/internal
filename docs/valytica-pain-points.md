## The ranked pain points

### 1 · Fulfilment Gap — *capacity constraint, demand ≫ supply*
The registered-valuer pool physically cannot clear the volume, so unserved reports are **yield leakage** — revenue that exists but can't be captured.

- ✅ **6,176** IBBI registered valuers nationally vs **~5M** property-loan valuations/yr
- 🧮 implied **~810 reports / valuer / yr** load
- 🟦 manual throughput capped at **1–2 reports / valuer / day**

**Valytica:** AI copilot lifts per-valuer throughput (AI does the desk work; valuer keeps inspection & sign-off).
**Impact:** 🟦 **3–4× throughput** (1–2 → 4–6/day) · desk time **6 hrs → 1.5 hrs** · converts unfilled demand into billable capacity.

### 2 · Valuation Variance & Compliance Exposure — *data integrity, audit risk*
Manual valuations carry wide, subjective variance and create **regulatory liability** when deficient.

- ✅ avg **absolute variance ~7.7%** vs sale price; only **~15%** land within ±5%, **~85%** within ±10%; **>90% biased upward** — driven by valuer "behavioural influences"
- ✅ a deficient report → **₹25k–₹5L fine + 3mo–2yr suspension** (IBBI / Companies Act §247)
- ✅ banks mandate **two** valuations on properties **>₹5 cr** (RBI/bank norms)

**Valytica:** grounded, **source-cited, standards-stated, audit-ready** output + cross-document checks — removing the top-3 IBBI deficiency reasons (undisclosed standards, undocumented sources, unjustified values).
**Impact:** 🟦 **98.4% accuracy, 0 hallucinations** vs ✅ industry AVMs at **2.4–2.8%** error (down from 10–15% five years ago) · ✅ ~**40% fewer errors** benchmark · 🧮 cuts the ~20% senior-review/rework premium.

### 3 · Turnaround-Time Bottleneck — *SLA adherence, critical-path delay*
Valuation sits on the **critical path** of loan disbursal; slow TAT delays disbursement and hurts conversion/CX.

- ✅ valuation assessment **5–7 days** (incl. physical inspection); total home-loan **7–15 days**; discrepancies/ROV add **7–10 more days**

**Valytica:** same-day report generation.
**Impact:** 🟦 TAT **2–3 days → same-day (~80%+ faster)** · removes valuation as the disbursal bottleneck.

### 4 · Cost-to-Serve & First-Time-Right — *rework rate, unit economics*
Manual effort + rework inflate **cost-to-serve** and erode margin.

- 🟦 **~6 hrs** desk work/report
- ✅ **15–20%** of cases need follow-up/correction (banking rework analog)

**Valytica:** automates extraction/cross-check/drafting; auto-checks raise **First-Time-Right**.
**Impact:** 🟦 cost-to-produce **₹2,000 → ₹220 (~89% / 9× lower)** · desk time **−75%** · higher FTR → lower rework.

### 5 · Peak-Load Inconsistency — *capacity utilisation, standardisation*
Month/quarter-end surge overwhelms fixed capacity; fatigue degrades quality; output varies across the panel.

- ✅ variance worsens under "behavioural influences"/fatigue; manual capacity can't flex to spikes

**Valytica:** elastic AI capacity absorbs the surge; standardised output across all panel valuers.
**Impact:** 🟦 same-day **at peak** · consistent, comparable reports bank-wide · no quality degradation under load.
